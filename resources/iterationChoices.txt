
Your survival depends on the quality of your choices.
What will you do next?

1) Eat.                     2) Drink water.     3) Go fishing.
4) Go hunting.              5) Go trapping.     6) Go foraging.
7) Work on your shelter.    8) Gather firewood. 9) Fetch water.
10) Boost your morale.      11) Rest.
